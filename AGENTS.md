## 1. Lilliput Seventh-day Adventist Church (Main Church)

### Historical Details
**Founding Story:**
- **Initiation:** 1974, through a conversation between Brother George Heavens and Miss Catherine Morrison
- **Location:** Lilliput District, Montego Bay, St. James
- **First Sunday School:** Held at Mr. Lawrence Lee's home in 1974
- **Foundation Members:** Sister Thelma Lee, Sister Leslie, Brother Heavens, Brother Renford McIntosh

**Key Milestones:**
- **1974:** Sunday School started
- **1975:** First zinc church structure built by the roadside
- **1985:** Church organized with 400 members after crusade by Pastor Oliphant
- **1988:** Hurricane Gilbert destroyed building; rebuilt with canvas
- **1993:** Relocated to current site after being forced out by Urban Development Corporation
- **Current:** Over 700 members

**Leadership History:**
- Founding Pioneer: Brother George Heavens
- Past Pastors: Pastors Burrows, Oliphant, Reid, Parchment, Duncan, Bygrave, Crew, Thomas, Williams, Sewell, Miller, Munroe, Taylor
- Current Pastor: Lataniel Hamilton (lhamilton@westjamaica.org)

**Contact & Location:**
- Address: Lot 200-202, Lilliput District, Montego Bay, Jamaica
- Website: https://lilliputsda.interamerica.org/
- Social Media: Facebook, YouTube, Instagram

**Source:** [Lilliput - About Us](https://lilliputsda.interamerica.org/about-us)

## 2. Power of Love SDA Church (Daughter Church of Lilliput SDA)

### Historical Details
**Founding:**
- **Organized:** November 2011
- **Origin:** Crusade conducted by Pastor Laundale Monroe in March-April 2011
- **Location:** Barrett Hall, originally a four-bedroom dwelling owned by Sister and Brother Exford Griffiths
- **First Members:** Over 100 persons met for Sabbath worship

**Key Milestones:**
- **November 12, 2011:** Congregation organized into a company
- **February 2012:** Two-week revival meetings by Pastor Astor Bowers; 15 persons baptized

**Leadership:**
- First Leadership: Elder Desmond Dawkins and small team

## 7. Historical Context: SDA in Jamaica

### Key Historical Information
**West Jamaica Conference History:**
- Organized: 1903 (as Jamaica Conference)
- Divided into East and West Jamaica Conferences: 1944
- Further division created Central Jamaica: 1962
- Current membership: Over 70,000
- Current churches: Over 220

**SDA Presence in St. James:**
- St. James is one of the original parishes where SDA work began
- Montego Bay is the capital and major urban center
- Lilliput district is a community within St. James

**Source:** West Jamaica Conference history page
